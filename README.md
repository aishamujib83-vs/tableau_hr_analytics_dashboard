# Tableau HR Analytics Dashboard
A Tableau dashboard project designed to analyze **Human Resources data**, providing both **summary-level insights** for decision-makers and **detailed employee records** for in-depth analysis.

This project features two key dashboards:
* **Summary View** – for high-level organizational insights.
* **Detailed View** – for exploring individual employee records.

## Project Overview
This Tableau project offers a complete view of workforce dynamics, visualizing HR metrics, demographic breakdowns, and salary trends. 

![*](docs/Chart_icon.png) **Dashboards**

**1. Summary View**<br/>
The Summary View is divided into three main analytical sections:

  **A. Overview**<br/>
  Provides a snapshot of key HR metrics, including:
  * Total number of **hired, active, and terminated** employees
  * Trend analysis of **hiring and termination over the years**
  * **Department and job-title-wise** employee distribution
  * Comparison of employees between **Headquarters vs. Branches**
  * **Geographical distribution** of employees by city and state

   **B. Demographics**<br/>
   Explores the composition of the workforce, covering:
   * **Gender ratio** across the company
   * Distribution of employees by **age group and education level**
   * Combined breakdown showing **employee count within each age and education category**

   **C. Income Analysis**<br/>
   Focuses on salary patterns, featuring:
   * **Average salary comparison** across education levels by gender
   * **Salary correlation** with age within each department
   * Insights into how education and experience influence **pay trends**

**2. Detailed View**<br/>
The Detailed View allows users to:
* Examine **individual employee records**
* Drill down into attributes like employee id, demographics, role, department, location,job status, and salary
* Facilitate data-driven employee reviews and case-specific HR queries

![*](docs/brain.png)**Key Insights**
* Identify hiring and termination trends over time
* Understand workforce diversity and qualification structure
* Detect salary imbalances across departments
* Support strategic workforce planning

![*](docs/Tools.png)**Tools & Technologies**
